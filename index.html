Plot

In a grand scheme of events, a troubled Anakin Skywalker finds himself constantly rejected by the Jedi Council. In hopes to become a Jedi Master at last, Anakin struggles to hang on to the beliefs of the Jedi Order. Meanwhile, Darth Sidious unveils himself and his master plan to rule the Galalxy with his new apprentice. Anakin struggles to stay loyal to the Jedi Order, or Join the Dark Side.
